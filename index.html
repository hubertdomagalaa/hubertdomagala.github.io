My Startups:

Dating App: "Meaningfull"

Meaningfull is an innovative dating app designed to foster genuine connections by prioritizing personality over appearance. Unlike typical dating apps like Tinder, Meaningfull takes a fresh approach by removing photos for the first week, allowing users to focus solely on personality traits and compatibility. Key Features:

  • AI-Powered Matching: Utilizing advanced AI algorithms, Meaningfull matches users based on their personality traits and values, ensuring deeper connections that go beyond surface-level attraction.
  • No Photos for the First Week: To promote meaningful interactions, users will remain anonymous for the first week, encouraging conversations based on shared interests.
  • Customization Options: After the initial week, users can specify a few appearance preferences to refine their matches while still emphasizing personality compatibility.
Vision: Our goal is to create a safe and inclusive space for users to find long-term partners, shifting the focus from visual attraction to emotional and intellectual connections.

Financial Bot

Introducing an innovative AI-powered financial advisor designed to revolutionize how investors approach stock trading and company evaluation. This bot leverages advanced algorithms and data analytics to provide users with real-time insights, helping them make informed investment decisions. Key Features:

  • Automated Stock Trading: The AI bot can execute trades on behalf of users based on pre-defined strategies, optimizing the trading process.
  • Comprehensive Company Analysis: The bot evaluates a company’s financial health and growth potential to determine if it’s a worthwhile investment opportunity.
  • Real-Time Insights: Users receive timely updates regarding stock performance and significant news events that could impact their investments.
Vision: The goal of this AI-powered financial advisor is to democratize access to sophisticated investment strategies, empowering users to make smarter investment choices.
